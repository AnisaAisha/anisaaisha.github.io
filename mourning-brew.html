Mourning Brew

Run your ghost cafe by taking orders, make them through minigames, then deliver and chat with your customers to learn more about them (and possibly yourself!)

  • Team Size: 20+
  • Tools: Unity, Blender, GitHub
  • Role(s): 3D Artist, Technical Artist, Gameplay Programmer

Contributed to the project by creating 3D assets, tech art, and visual effects, and by programming ghost movement and minigame features. Tech Art and Visual Effects

Created visual effects using Unity's Shader Graph and particle systems, incorporating color blending, noise distortion, and animations.

  • Developed a stylized blowtorch flame shader for the Creme Brulee minigame, combining noise patterns with gradients.
  • Designed a tea liquid shader with subtle distortion and a soft steam effect for the Tea Pouring minigame.
Gameplay Programming

Muffin Minigame: Designed and implemented an interactive muffin-making minigame that simulates the baking process within a tray.

  • Configured particle systems to simulate realistic muffin batter pouring, with collision detection.
  • Implemented bowl tilting and movement mechanics to allow controlled pouring into individual muffin slots.
  • Integrated individual progress bars for each muffin slot to visually track filling progress.
  • Used orthographic camera projection to achieve a clean 2D-style aesthetic within a 3D environment.

Donut Minigame: Designed and implemented an interactive muffin-making minigame that simulates the baking process within a tray.

  • Implemented tongs movement mechanics to pick up donuts (similar to crane games).
  • Created compound colliders for donuts and tongs for collision detection.
  • Configured collision mechanics such that the donuts are only picked up if the tongs are correctly positioned, otherwise they fall.

Additional Feature: Developed idle floating animations for ghost characters in the main scene.
