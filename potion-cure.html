Potion Cure

Raat-ki-Raani, a cursed witch, has a prophecy that brings her to an abandoned and dilapidated apothecary- its owner trapped inside a mirror, buried under the rubble. Little by little, the witch must learn the ropes of running the apothecary and gently bring it back to life. She must discover hidden stories, master her craft and grow as a witch; only then will she get the key to break her curse.

Game Menus and UI

Designed UI menus for the main and settings menu, and implemented in-game. Settings menu included configuration of in-game audio and SFX.

Level Manager

Created level manager screen where the players can buy items using the in-game currency. The bought items replaced the previously broken items displayed on screen.
